Comprehensive Recovery: Enhancing Movement, Strength, and Cognitive Function Together

Hand Function (Gross & Fine Motor)

Regain strength, precision, and control in everyday hand movements.

Happy Moves includes interactive games that develop both fine and gross motor skills of the hand. From precise finger movements to larger grip and reach actions, our activities are designed to improve strength, coordination, and control — helping users regain independence in daily tasks.

Restore flexibility and ease in upper-body movements.

Our motion-guided therapy games focus on increasing flexibility, reducing stiffness, and restoring safe, confident movement of the neck and shoulders. These exercises support better posture, upper-body coordination, and comfort in movement.

Neck & Shoulder Mobility

Cognitive Enhancement

Boost focus, memory, and mental agility through movement.

We integrate cognitive challenges into physical tasks to boost mental agility. Through games involving memory, attention, problem-solving, and sequencing, patients stay mentally active while progressing physically — supporting a holistic recovery.

Strengthen the foundation for balance and posture.

Happy Moves includes targeted movements that activate the core and stabilize the trunk. These activities help improve balance, coordination, and posture — essential for standing, walking, and preventing falls.

Trunk Stability & Core Control
